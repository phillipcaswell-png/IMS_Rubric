## v1.7.3 — Phase Completion Summary

### Phase 1 — Constitutional Foundation (v1.0.0–v1.4.0)
Established governance before functionality. Proved that constitutional
boundaries could survive real historical validation. Answered:
"How should Athena make decisions?"

### Phase 2 — Evidence Intelligence (v1.5.0–v1.7.2)
Built the path from raw evidence to analyst-ready synthesis. Proved that
Theia could extract, Athena could synthesize, and neither could cross into
governed territory. Answered: "How does Athena transform evidence into
decision support?"

### Phase 3 — Product Identity (v1.7.3)
Separated external brand from internal architecture. Product is Athena.
Positioning is Governed Investment Intelligence. Internal agents retain
their constitutional names. Answered: "How does Athena present itself
without compromising its architecture?"

### Athena Reference Case 001 (pending)

Validation Case 002 — Eastman Kodak (2000-09-30) — will become the first
fully governed decision executed under the complete architecture.

Upon completion it becomes a permanent regression baseline for:

- Evidence staging
- Theia extraction
- Athena synthesis
- Analyst scoring sequence
- Governed decision record
- Mnemosyne outcome attribution

Future architectural changes must preserve the explainability,
traceability, and reproducibility of this reference case.

This reference case validates the constitutional process — not investment
performance. The broader research question, "Does the governed process
produce better decisions than unstructured analysis?", remains an empirical
question to be evaluated across the growing Validation Portfolio rather
than inferred from a single case.
